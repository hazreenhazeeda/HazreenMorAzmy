Hi, I want to share about my life

Business

I started my business during Form 5 by selling handsocks at school. After finishing my SPM, I decided to continue my business by becoming a dropship from NSMC to do business more widely using the online platform. After a few months, I decided to register for another brand, Heeladina. It gives me a new experience of selling scarves and t-shirt. After six months, I looked to another brand, Dyanadise, and became dropship and agent because I always buy the scarf from them. I feel so thankful because I got a lot of experience and business knowledge from these three brands.

Passion

I have an interest in creating posters and editing videos. When I entered Publisiti and Multimedia Bureau in the IPMM club, I realized that. From that, I tried to make many posters according to a given theme. In addition, I often joined poster competitions, and I once won second place for Drilling Poster conducted by UTP. Lastly, I once took a job making MPP posters for one of the MPP candidates.
